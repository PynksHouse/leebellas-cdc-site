About Lee-Bella’s

A warm, home-like environment that supports working families with reliable care when childcare is hardest to find.

Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, and dependable 24-hour childcare environment that supports working families, promotes early childhood development, and ensures every child feels cared for, protected, and at home.

Who We Serve

  • Single-parent households
  • Dual-income families
  • Healthcare and hospital staff
  • First responders and public safety employees
  • Warehouse, logistics, and transportation workers
  • Hospitality and service industry workers
🌙

Transportation Program

Optional transportation helps families without reliable vehicles and supports overnight workers who need safe, dependable drop-off and pickup. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Features
