About Lee-Bella's

Lee-Bella's No Place Like Home Child Development Center is a proposed 24-hour childcare center created to support working families in Gwinnett County.

Our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing, dependable environment where children can learn, rest, grow, and feel at home while their parents work.

We are especially focused on families with nontraditional schedules, including healthcare workers, first responders, warehouse employees, hospitality staff, and other essential workers.
